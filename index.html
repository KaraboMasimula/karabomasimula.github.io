Welcome

Vox Records is an independent record label based in Pretoria, Gauteng. Our aim is to support and promote emerging and established artists across the UK; releasing music, organising tours and securing press coverage for the acts signed to the label. Music distribution to all major online stores secured via Ditto Music, including iTunes, Spotify, Apple Music, Tidal, Deezer and Google Play.Artist management provided by label owner Thabo Sithole, a successful independent musician with over 10 years’ experience working in the industry.
